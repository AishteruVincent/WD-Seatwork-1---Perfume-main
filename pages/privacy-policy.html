Privacy Policy

At Allurea, we value the privacy of our visitors and customers. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use our services, such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details. We also collect information through cookies to improve your user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information may be used for the following purposes:

  • Processing and completing your orders
  • Improving our website and services
  • Sending promotional emails and updates
  • Responding to customer service inquiries

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no security system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or the safety of others
  • With service providers who assist us in running our website

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also opt-out of receiving promotional emails by following the unsubscribe instructions in our communications.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@allurea.com.
